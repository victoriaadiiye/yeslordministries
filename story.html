The Story

A life of saying Yes — to missions, to courage, to the God who never stops calling.

Called at Twelve

Some people spend their whole lives searching for their purpose. For the founder of Yes Lord Ministries, the call came early — at the age of twelve, a clear and unmistakable invitation into missions. It would be the first of many moments where the only right answer was: Yes, Lord.

That childhood yes set in motion a lifetime of adventure, faith, and service — taking her across borders and cultures, into places of joy and hardship alike, always carried by the conviction that God opens doors when we choose to walk through them.

"I believe God is always inviting us to say Yes to join Him in the places where He is at work."

A Life of Yes

The journey has not always been easy. There have been seasons of uncertainty, closed doors, and moments where faith had to press against fear. But through every chapter, the invitation remained the same — to trust, to step forward, and to watch what God would do.

Yes to forgiveness, even when it cost something. Yes to loving those who are difficult to love. Yes to believing in miracles when circumstances said otherwise. Yes to speaking truth in a culture that drowns it out. These are not just stories from the past — they are the continuing testimony of a life surrendered to God's direction.

Yes Lord Ministries

Yes Lord Ministries exists to carry that same message forward — that God meets our courage with His faithfulness. Through speaking, writing, and community, the ministry brings a word of Hope and Encouragement to people navigating their own seasons of uncertainty, inviting them into their own story of faith.

Whether you are standing at a crossroads, carrying a weight you were not meant to carry alone, or simply wondering what God might be asking of you next — there is room here for your yes.

"When we say Yes to Him, He opens doors and opportunities for us to experience life to the fullest."

The Book

Yes, Lord! Navigating Faith with Courage gathers these stories into a book written for anyone ready to say yes to the life God is offering them. It is part memoir, part invitation — an honest account of adventure, obedience, and the God who proves Himself faithful at every turn.
