Yes, Lord!

Navigating Faith with Courage

Yes, Lord! Navigating Faith with Courage tells the story of a life called at 12 years of age into missions — and the courage God met her with as she continued to say yes to His invitations.

God is always inviting us to join Him in the places where He is at work. When we say Yes to Him, He opens doors and opportunities for us to experience life to the fullest.

While engaging the reader in adventurous, suspenseful stories, this book circles back to your own story — what might God be inviting you to say yes to? Yes to forgiveness. Yes to loving those who are difficult to love. Yes to believing in Him for a miracle. Yes to choosing to trust Him through life's storms.
